Ferrari F40

Specifications

  • Engine: 2.9L Twin-Turbo V8
  • Top Speed: 201 mph (324 km/h)
  • 0-60 mph: 4.2 seconds

About the F40

The Ferrari F40 was the last car personally approved by Enzo Ferrari. Built to celebrate the company's 40th anniversary, it was a pure, lightweight, and uncompromising supercar. With no driver aids and extensive use of carbon fiber, it was the first production car to break the 200 mph barrier.
