Ford Mustang

Specifications (Mach 1)

  • Engine: 5.0L V8
  • Top Speed: 166 mph (267 km/h)
  • 0-60 mph: 4.3 seconds

About the Mustang

The Ford Mustang created the "pony car" class when it launched in 1964. It was an instant success, offering style, performance, and affordability. From the original fastback to modern-day GTs and Shelbys, the Mustang has remained an icon of American muscle and automotive freedom.
