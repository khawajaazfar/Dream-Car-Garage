Specifications

About the McLaren F1

The McLaren F1 is often considered the greatest supercar ever built. It was a no-compromise vision from designer Gordon Murray, featuring a central driving position, a gold-lined engine bay, and a naturally-aspirated V12. It held the record for the world's fastest production car for over a decade.
