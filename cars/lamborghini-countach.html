Lamborghini Countach

Specifications (LP400)

  • Engine: 4.0L V12
  • Top Speed: 179 mph (288 km/h)
  • 0-60 mph: 5.4 seconds

About the Countach

The Lamborghini Countach is the original "poster car." Its dramatic wedge shape, cab-forward design, and iconic "scissor doors" defined the supercar look for decades. It was a visual shockwave in the 1970s and remains one of the most outrageous and recognized car designs of all time.
