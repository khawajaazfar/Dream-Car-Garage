Bugatti Veyron 16.4

Specifications (Super Sport)

  • Engine: 8.0L Quad-Turbo W16
  • Top Speed: 267 mph (431 km/h)
  • 0-60 mph: 2.4 seconds

About the Veyron

The Bugatti Veyron was a moonshot project for Volkswagen Group. It was designed to be the fastest, most powerful, and most luxurious production car ever made. With 1,001 horsepower (and more in later versions) and 10 radiators, it was a monumental feat of engineering that pushed the limits of physics.
