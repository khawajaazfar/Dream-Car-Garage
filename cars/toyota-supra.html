Toyota Supra MK IV

Specifications (Turbo)

  • Engine: 3.0L Twin-Turbo 2JZ-GTE
  • Top Speed: 155 mph (250 km/h)
  • 0-60 mph: 4.6 seconds

About the Supra MK IV

The A80, or "Mark 4" Supra, is a legend of 1990s Japanese engineering. Its heart is the legendary 2JZ-GTE engine, a motor so robust that tuners have famously modified it to produce well over 1,000 horsepower. Its iconic status was cemented by its starring role in the *Fast and Furious* films.
