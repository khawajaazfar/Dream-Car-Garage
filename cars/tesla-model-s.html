About the Model S Plaid

The Tesla Model S Plaid redefined what a four-door sedan could be. With over 1,000 horsepower, its instant electric torque delivers acceleration that rivals (and often beats) the most expensive hypercars in the world. It combines record-breaking performance with long-range usability.
