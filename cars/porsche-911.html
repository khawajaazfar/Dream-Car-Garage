Specifications (911 Turbo S)

About the 911

The Porsche 911 is the quintessential sports car. Since 1964, its iconic silhouette, rear-engine layout, and "driver-first" philosophy have remained. It is the benchmark for performance, handling, and everyday usability, evolving over generations while never losing its soul.
