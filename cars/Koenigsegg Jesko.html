About the Jesko

The Koenigsegg Jesko represents the next evolution in hypercar performance. Built for extreme speed and precision, it features advanced aerodynamics, a lightweight carbon fiber chassis, and an engine capable of producing up to 1,600 horsepower on E85 fuel. It is designed to break records and redefine automotive engineering.
